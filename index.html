About

This is an unofficial desktop client that brings the messenger.com app to your desktop. The functionality is identical, because the client embeds the official website. You can send media and files, make voice and video calls, and enable desktop notifications with sounds.

Dock notifications badge for the OS X version of the app is a work-in-progress.
